As Special Warfare Operator First Class in the Navy's most Elite Unit DevGru; you now have access to the full arsenal of SOCOM JSOC Armory. Your Callsign will be Blue 5, and you are expected to report to your Team Lead Master Chief Clint Torrsen immediately.  
  
NOTES:  
\- Creates a new in-game trader that has existing EFT Items; which are consisent to SOCOM standard weapons. Trader is Level 4 by default.

\- Items are on average 50% cheaper than reatail. This will be payroll deducted from your hazard pay.

\- Some items are a larger volume discount depending on the purchase negotiaion by JSOC Procurement Buyers.   
  
DEPENDENCIES:  
\- Mod works with no dependencies, however Keano Traders Scrolling makes a nice way to scroll through your traders without being blocked by in-game overlays and banners. [Kaeno-TraderScrolling](https://hub.sp-tarkov.com/files/file/1508-kaeno-traderscrolling/?highlight=kaeno)  
  
INSTALLATION:  
\- Download and extract to \[SPT-AKI Directory\]\\user\\mods folder.  
\- If you want to use the "hardcore" pricing structure provided by [Srispt](https://hub.sp-tarkov.com/user/75172-srispt/), simply navigate to \[SPT-AKI Directory\]\\user\\mods\\SOCOM\\db\\ and rename the "assort.json" to somethine else such as "assort.old.json" and rename the "assort\_hardcore.json" to assort.json".
